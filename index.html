Wait, What is Crohn's Disease?

Well, it's not good...

Crohn's is a chronic inflammatory condition of the gastrointestinal tract -- which a really long, really important part of your body. If food flows through it from when you eat to when you 💩, it is a part of the GI tract. Now imagine if that tract has inflammation and that inflamamation does not subside - it's chronic. Not ideal right?

There is no cure to crohn's but there are treatment options to help with flare-ups and prevent relapses... but half of all patients with crohn's require surgery in the first ten years following diagnosis, and 20% are admitted to the hospital each year.
